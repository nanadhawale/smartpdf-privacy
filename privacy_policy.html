SmartPDF Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the SmartPDF app.

2. Data We Collect

  • Device identifier (Android ID) – only for analytics.
  • PDF files you choose to compress, edit or share – stored locally only.
  • Optional crash‑report data (if you enable “Send error reports”).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data solely to provide the app’s core features (PDF processing) and to improve stability. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

4. Your Rights

You may delete any stored files at any time from the app’s settings screen. You can also revoke analytics by toggling the switch in Settings.

5. Contact

For any questions, email: privacy@smartpdf.app
